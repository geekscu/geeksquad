How to Schedule an Official Geek Squad Appointment

  1. Go to the Geek Squad scheduling portal: Use the appointment web portal to start. (Official: bestbuy.com/services/triage/schedule) [replace link location as appropriate]
  2. Choose your service type: Select computer repair, appliance setup, smart home consultation, or device troubleshooting.
  3. Enter ZIP code/location: Find the nearest Best Buy or in-home service area.
  4. Pick your preferred appointment method: Appointments can be in-store, in-home, or remote/virtual.
  5. Select the date and time: Browse slots and pick what's best for you. Weekdays and midday often have the most availability.
  6. Describe your device/problem details: Let your agent know exactly what to prepare for.
  7. Complete contact info: Fill out name, email, and any additional preferences.
  8. Confirm and receive confirmation: A confirmation email/SMS will include your appointment number, time, and any prep steps.
Tip: For fastest service, have your device details ready and check confirmation email for special instructions.

How to Manage, Reschedule, or Cancel a Geek Squad Appointment

  1. Visit the scheduling portal and log in or enter your appointment confirmation code.
  2. Select to reschedule, cancel, or view appointment details.
  3. For changes, pick new dates/times; a new confirmation is sent.
