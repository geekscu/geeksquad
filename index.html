How to Schedule Your Appointment Online

  1. Visit Geek Squad’s Official Appointment Page. Start booking on the official Best Buy Geek Squad services portal.
  2. Select your service type. Choose between device repair, installation, setup, or tech support.
  3. Enter your ZIP code or city. Locate your nearest in-home or in-store Geek Squad appointment option.
  4. Pick your preferred method: In-store, in-home, or remote sessions available for most services.
  5. Choose your date and time slot. Check open slots and submit your preferred appointment schedule.
  6. Describe issue and device details. Provide useful info to help agents arrive prepared.
  7. Fill out your contact details. Input name, email, and optional preferences for confirmations.
  8. Review and confirm. Submit to get your confirmation code and prep instructions by email/SMS.
Pro Tip: Have model numbers, error messages, or photos ready to speed up diagnostics.

Manage, Reschedule, or Cancel Your Appointment

  1. Log into the Geek Squad online portal using your confirmation code.
  2. Select the appointment you want to reschedule or cancel.
  3. Pick a new time/date, or cancel by following instructions onscreen.
  4. Receive updates and confirmation by email/SMS.
You can manage all appointments anytime before your reserved window. Cancelling early helps other customers find timely support.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to make an appointment for Geek Squad?

Yes, booking ensures a certified agent is ready and wait times are reduced for all service types.

Can I change my appointment after booking?

Easily! Return to the portal at any time to reschedule or cancel your appointment using your code.

What happens if I miss my appointment?

You may rebook anytime. Missing means your time slot opens for others—plan early to reserve a convenient schedule.

Are data recovery and virus removal available?

Yes. Book Data Recovery and Virus & Spyware Removal for PCs, Macs, or tablets using the same process described above.
